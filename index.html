Magical Healing Crystals

Your mother has a terrible illness and the only cure is to use these magical healing rocks in some kind of weird alchemic psuedoscience. You need the appropriate amount of each magical rock to cure the cancer. Each rock has a number value and you need to reach the magical cure number. Clicking each rock will add to your total and each rock will have a randomly determined value. Be careful how much you add!
